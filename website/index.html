DiscAbilities

Equip music discs. Activate powerful abilities. Each disc comes with a unique active skill and always-on passives that change how you play.

Single Disc Slot 21 Unique Discs Passive + Active Abilities Paper 1.21
How It Works
01
Find a Disc
Obtain any of the 21 ability discs in the game world or from an admin.
02
Equip It
Right-click the disc in your hand to equip it. Your passives activate immediately.
Right Click
03
Activate
Press your swap-hand key to fire the disc's ability. Mind the cooldown.
F Key
04
Withdraw
Use /withdraw to unequip your disc and get it back in your inventory.
/withdraw
Commands
/getdisc <disc>
Give yourself a music disc by name.
Admin Only
/withdraw
Unequip your current disc back to your inventory.
/resetcooldowns [player]
Reset all disc cooldowns for yourself or a target player.
Admin Only
/getitem <material> [amount]
Give yourself any Minecraft item by material name.
Admin Only
